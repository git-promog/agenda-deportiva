## Executive Summary

GuíaSports is a sports TV and streaming guide targeting Mexican sports fans, addressing a genuine market pain point: the fragmentation of broadcasting rights across multiple platforms (Disney+, ESPN, ViX, SKY, FOX, etc.). The site provides clear utility by aggregating where-to-watch information for fútbol, NBA, MLB, F1, Champions League, and other popular sports.

**Biggest Strength:** Clear, focused value proposition with comprehensive multi-platform coverage. The site immediately communicates its purpose—"find where to watch sports in Mexico"—and delivers on that promise with a clean, mobile-responsive interface.

**Biggest Gap:** Critical trust and engagement deficits. The site lacks any social proof (no user counts, testimonials, or media mentions), has no user capture mechanism (no newsletter, push notifications, or social media), and suffers from technical SEO issues including broken article pages and an incomplete sitemap.

**Top 3 Actions That Would Move the Needle Most:**

1. **Implement Email Newsletter + Push Notifications** — Capture users before they leave. A daily sports schedule email could generate 2,000-5,000 subscribers per 100,000 visitors, driving 3x return visits.

2. **Fix Technical SEO Issues** — Article pages show "Noticia no encontrada" in title tags, and the sitemap only indexes 6 URLs. Fixing these could double organic traffic within 60 days.

3. **Add Affiliate Partnerships** — Partner with ViX, Disney+, ESPN for referral commissions. The Mexican streaming market is growing rapidly, and GuíaSports is positioned to capture affiliate revenue.

**Estimated Revenue Impact of Full Implementation:** $4,000-12,000/month additional revenue through affiliate partnerships, improved ad rates from higher engagement, and sponsored event placements.
