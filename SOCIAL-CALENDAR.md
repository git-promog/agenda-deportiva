# Social Media Content Calendar: GuíaSports
**Date:** April 2, 2026
**Period:** April 2026 — 30-Day Calendar
**Platforms:** Twitter/X, Instagram, Facebook

---

## Brand Context

- **Brand:** GuíaSports
- **Tagline:** "La guía deportiva más rápida de México"
- **Mission:** Ayudar a los aficionados mexicanos a encontrar dónde ver sus deportes favoritos
- **Audience:** Mexican sports fans, 18-45, mobile-first, who struggle to find where to watch sports across fragmented streaming platforms
- **Voice:** Helpful, passionate, fast, accessible — like a friend who always knows where the game is on
- **Goal:** Build brand awareness, drive traffic to guiasports.com, establish authority as the go-to sports guide for Mexico
- **Unique Positioning:** Platform-agnostic — we cover ALL platforms (ViX, Disney+, ESPN, SKY, TV abierta), not just one

---

## Platform Strategy

| Platform | Priority | Frequency | Focus |
|----------|----------|-----------|-------|
| **Twitter/X** | Primary | 2-3x daily | Real-time updates, event alerts, engagement |
| **Instagram** | Primary | 1-2x daily | Visual schedules, carousels, Reels, Stories |
| **Facebook** | Secondary | 1x daily | Community, event listings, older demographic |

---

## Content Pillars

### Pillar 1: ¿Dónde Ver? (Educational) — 35% of content
Daily posts answering the core question: where to watch tonight's events. Include channel lists, streaming platforms, and times.

### Pillar 2: Alertas Deportivas (Timely/Urgent) — 25% of content
Breaking updates, lineup changes, last-minute channel changes, live event reminders.

### Pillar 3: Comunidad Deportiva (Engagement) — 20% of content
Polls, questions, debates, predictions, "quién gana hoy" discussions.

### Pillar 4: Entre Bastidores (Behind-the-Scenes/Brand) — 10% of content
How the guide works, team picks, platform comparisons, "cómo elegimos los canales".

### Pillar 5: Contenido Premium (Promotional) — 10% of content
Newsletter signup, featured events, website CTAs, "visita GuíaSports" prompts.
